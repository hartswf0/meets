About This Research

This archive documents a practice-based dissertation investigating how thick prompts—densely layered, context-rich instructions—transform human-AI collaboration from mechanical query-response into genuine creative dialogue.

The work bridges media theory (McLuhan), cybernetics (Bateson), and interpretive anthropology (Geertz) to develop new frameworks for understanding viscous, meaning-rich exchanges between humans and AI systems.
